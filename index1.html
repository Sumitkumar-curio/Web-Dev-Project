About the Symposium

The 13th Asian Symposium on Intense Laser Science (ASILS13) is the latest in a series of highly successful symposia, bringing together leading researchers from Asia and around the world. This symposium focuses on cutting-edge research in the exciting field of ultra-short, intense laser-matter interactions and their various applications. The primary objective of ASILS13 is to foster collaborations and share recent advances in the field of intense laser science. The symposium serves as a platform for scientists and researchers to explore new opportunities in the study of ultra-short, high-intensity laser interactions with matter.

Topics of the Symposium

High to Ultra-high Laser Intensity Science Attosecond Science and High Harmonic Generation
Laser-Driven Particle Acceleration Ultrafast Plasma Dynamics
THz Generation and Applications Computational Plasma Physics
